Quilts for Fire Survivors

CZU Lightning Fire Quilts is a quilt drive for fire survivors in the CZU Lightning Complex Fire in Santa Cruz county. Our goal is to provide a quilt for every bed in each destroyed household to all fire survivors who have lost their homes.
